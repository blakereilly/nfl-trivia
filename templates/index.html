How to Play FumbLe

1. A player's position and career stats will be displayed. Your goal is to guess the NFL player's name.

2. You get 4 guesses. If you are stuck, you can use the "Get Hint" button but it will take away one of your guesses.

3. You can guess a player by their full name or last name only. Entries are not case sensitive.

4. Guess correctly or run out of guesses to end the game.

Player Data from Pro Football Reference
